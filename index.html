Tove's Life

Childhood

Tove Jansson was raised by artist parents as a child. Against this background, she became interested in art from a young age.

Early Artistic Life

She studied art at an art school in Stockholm. After graduation, she drew many illustrations to various paper media.

The Moomins

Her famous character the Moomin often appeared in the magazine illustrations she serialized. It was exported overseas, and the Moomin became famous.

Late Life

The Moomin franchise was a huge success. She produced various works about the Moomins and spent her later years on the island with her partner Tuulikki.
